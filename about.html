The concept

About VisionGlass

A front-end study in spatial interfaces — how far plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript can go toward feeling like glass in a room, without a headset or a framework.

Why glass

Glassmorphism is usually a flat blur behind a card. VisionGlass tries to earn the metaphor: real depth via parallax, a highlight that tracks light the way a physical pane would, and reflections at the border edge.

Why no framework

Every interaction here — the dock, the cursor, the parallax, the particle field — is native browser APIs: Pointer Events, Intersection Observer, Canvas, and CSS custom properties doing the heavy lifting.
